Welcome to PREMIER TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT Limited

Today's busy transport environment demands that highway and road maintenance is undertaken quickly, efficiently and SAFELY!

PREMIER TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT Limited is a specialist traffic management sub-contractor that provides high quality temporary traffic management services to the construction, civil engineering and utilities sector nationwide.  

Established in 2000 with facilities in County Durham, Doncaster, Northumberland, Penrith and Merseyside, our aim is to provide a high quality service at a competitive price and to EXCEED the exacting standards of performance required by Highways work.  

Premier Traffic Management Limited is Quality Assured to BS EN ISO 9001:2008 and is a member of the Traffic Management Contractors Association (TMCA), Contractors Health & Safety Assessment Scheme (CHAS), Utilities Vender Database (UVDB Achilles), PICS Consortium, Construction Line and Safe Contractor.
