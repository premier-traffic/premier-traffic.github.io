Specialist Traffic Management Vehicles

All TM vehicles comply with the Temporary Traffic Management on High Speed Roads – Good Working Practices (SPITTMO).

  • Reflective Markings.

  • CCTV & Audio Warning for Reversing.

  • Roof Mounted & Rear Mounted Beacons.

Lorry Mounted Crash Cushion (LMCC)

  • Minimum 10 Tonne Gross Vehicle Weight.

  • Automatic Brake Activation System..

  • All seats fitted with head restraints and inertia seat belts.

  • Light arrow board and sign.

  • CCTV & audio warning for reversing.
